Character Sketch

Spotlights that overhang past the sidewalk flood onto the road in the evening. Suddenly, the white puddles of light on the asphalt get ripped apart by a racing shadow. A long black sedan curves around the corner and zips under lights while screeching to a halt in front of a fabric canopy over the sidewalk. The drivers door flicks open into the road, and out pops a muscular black man steps.His long black tactical boots clunk along the cement sidewalk as he casually walks around to the back door of the car. The door clicks open and the car's stereo turns silent. A ruff tan fedora pops out from behind the jet black tinted windows. Below the sparkly black paint of the door, two pointed tan Gucci shoes drop to the ground. A man’s head appears with his navy blue coat covers his mouth. He stands shorter than his driver and lacks the visual strength. Yet despite his laid back stance and smirk, his presence causes a the wind to run in terror and light poles to shake in shock. The car door closes, and the man casually walks under the canopy. His driver opens a door at the building and the two of them enter calmly. It’s a restaurant, extremely fancy yet surprisingly empty for the time. A host stands inside and nods to the driver and the two of them pass by. They walk through to the back of the restaurant, passing empty tables along the way and muttering words to one another. The driver slows down, and the man with the fedora paces up to a circular table in the corner. There is one person at the table, eating a plate of lobster tale and caviar. Before they can get a word off, the man with the fedora drops his hat down and shakes his head. His hand reaches to his coat and slides out a shiny colt. The table rattles in terror and the colt fires twice. The man then places the colt back in his jacket, tops his head with the fedora, and walks away.
